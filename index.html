About LINC Hub

LINC Hub is a professional freelance partner network and project hub designed to help companies identify top talent, fill strategic positions, and develop high-performing teams efficiently.

We connect businesses and project teams with experienced professionals in HR, management, and leadership through a commission-based system, ensuring that every project is executed professionally and at scale.

  • Professional Freelance Partner Network
  • Project Hub for HR, Management, and Leadership
  • Commission-Based Business Model
  • Efficiency & Professionalism at Scale

Vendors

We collaborate with trusted vendors who share our commitment to professionalism, transparency, and quality. Vendors are key partners in delivering sustainable HR and business solutions.

Clients

We help clients find the right professionals, onboard talent, and build sustainable systems that ensure growth and long-term collaboration. Our only upfront fee is the Booking Fee (for screening, filtering, and candidate sharing). Partnership fee is applied only after project completion and successful onboarding.
